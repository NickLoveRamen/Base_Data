Bloody Mary

1 1/2 Oz. Vodka
3 Oz. Tomato Juice
1 Dash Lemon Juice
1/2 Tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
2 Or 3 Drops Tabasco Sauce
1 Wedge Lime


Mixing Instructions:

Shake all ingredients (except lime wedge) 
with ice and strain into an old-fashioned 
glass over ice cubes. Add salt and pepper 
to taste. Add the wedge of lime and serve.
