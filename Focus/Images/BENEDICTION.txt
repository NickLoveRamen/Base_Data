   BENEDICTION
                                         
2.5-3oz Stoli Gold
Splash (.25oz) of B&B
Splash of Frangelico
                                    
Pour a splash of Frangelico into
a chilled cocktail glass.  Swirl it 
around to coat the glass and spill 
out the excess. 
                                      
Combine vodka and B&B in a shaker 
and shake briefly.
                                     
Strain into the cocktail glass
                                       
garnish with a twist.
