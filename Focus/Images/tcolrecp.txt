Tom Collins

2 Oz. Gin
1 Oz. Lemon Juice
1 Tsp. Superfine Sugar
3 Oz. Club Soda
1 Maraschino Cherry
1 Orange Slice


Mixing Instructions:

In a shaker half-filled with ice cubes, 
combine the gin, lemon juice, and sugar. 
Shake well. Strain into a collins glass 
almost filled with ice cubes. Add the 
club soda. Stir and garnish with the 
cherry and the orange slice.
