Rum Punch

2 Oz. Light Rum
1 Oz. Lemon Juice
1/2 Tsp. Superfine Sugar
1 Orange Slice
1/2 Tsp Grenadine
Maraschino Cherry


Mixing Instructions:

In a shaker half-filled with 
ice cubes, combine the rum, 
lemon juice, Grenadine
and sugar. 

Shake well. Strain into a sour glass 
and garnish with the orange slice 
and the cherry.
